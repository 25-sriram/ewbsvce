Currently Ongoing Projects

Smart Ambulance Traffic Control (AUTOGREEN)

An EWB project designed to give ambulances a clear path by turning red lights green when they approach. The system reduces delays, speeds up emergency response, and helps save lives, with future scope in IoT and AI-based traffic optimization.

Microplastic Remedation

Our research addresses LDPE microplastic pollution using an integrated biodegradation approach that merges fungal degradation with supplementary biological agents. This synergistic method enhances plastic breakdown efficiency, showing clear improvements in surface modification and mass reduction. It presents a sustainable, eco-friendly pathway for microplastic remediation and future large-scale environmental applications.
